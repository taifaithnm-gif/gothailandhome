# G-INVESTMENT — ROI Wording Policy

**Gate:** G-INVESTMENT  
**Document ID:** GINV-ROI-V1  
**Status:** APPROVED  
**Approval date:** 2026-07-20

## 1. Purpose

Controls any mention of ROI, yield, or return language on investment surfaces.

## 2. Policy

| Term | Allowed use | Forbidden use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ROI | **None** in Phase 1 investment guide | Any numeric or comparative ROI statement |
| Yield | **None** | Rental yield, gross yield, net yield |
| Return | Only in negation: "does not provide return forecasts" | "expected return", "annual return" |
| Investment | "investment education", "investment discovery framing" | "investment recommendation" |
| Screening | "entry screening by published price" (homepage-aligned) | "investment screening score" |

## 3. UI copy on guide route

Required visible labels (localized):

- Guide type: "Educational investment guide"
- Boundary: "Not investment advice"
- Forecast boundary: per `G_INVESTMENT_FORECAST_DISCLAIMER.md`

## 4. Cross-surface consistency

Homepage `investmentBody` and district investment summaries must remain consistent with this policy (no yield language). Investment guide must not contradict those boundaries.

**APPROVED** under decision **GINV-D-004**.
