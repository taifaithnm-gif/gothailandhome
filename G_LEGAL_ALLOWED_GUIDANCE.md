# G-LEGAL — Allowed Legal Guidance

**Gate:** G-LEGAL  
**Document ID:** GLEG-ALLOWED-V1  
**Status:** APPROVED  
**Approval date:** 2026-07-20

## 1. Purpose

Lists guidance classes the approved legal guide may include verbatim or in localized equivalent.

## 2. Allowed guidance classes

| Class | Example (EN) | Condition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jurisdiction | "This guide describes general information about property rules in Thailand." | Must display jurisdiction label |
| Non-advice boundary | "This is general information, not legal advice for your situation." | Mandatory |
| Condominium framing | "Foreign buyers often review condominium foreign-ownership quota rules with qualified counsel." | No eligibility conclusion for a reader |
| Professional referral | "Consult a qualified Thai lawyer before signing contracts or transferring funds." | Mandatory |
| Platform role | "GoThailandHome is not a law firm and does not provide legal services." | Must match platform_process |
| Source citation | Link to Land Department or official government pages when stating public rules | Required for factual legal statements |

## 3. Factual statements

Factual statements about Thai property law must:

- Cite an allowed `government` source with `verified_at`
- Use hedged language ("generally", "often", "may")
- Avoid telling a specific reader they qualify or do not qualify
