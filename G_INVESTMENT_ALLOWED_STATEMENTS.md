# G-INVESTMENT — Allowed Investment Statements

**Gate:** G-INVESTMENT  
**Document ID:** GINV-ALLOWED-V1  
**Status:** APPROVED  
**Approval date:** 2026-07-20

## 1. Purpose

Lists statement classes the approved investment guide may include verbatim or in localized equivalent.

## 2. Allowed statement classes

| Class | Example (EN) | Condition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Platform role | "GoThailandHome is a property discovery marketplace, not the listing agent." | Must match platform_process facts |
| Listing provenance | "Published prices come from verified public source pages and may change." | Must not imply official developer pricing |
| Due diligence framing | "Review source links, verification labels, and freshness dates before relying on a listing." | Educational only; no outcome promise |
| Process pointers | "Use Find My Home for a private buyer brief." | Must link to approved routes |
| Non-forecast boundary | "This guide does not provide yield, ROI, or return forecasts." | Mandatory in body or disclaimer block |
| Professional referral | "Consult qualified tax, legal, and financial professionals for decisions." | Mandatory; not a substitute for advice |

## 3. Numeric claims

- **Allowed:** Counts of listings or projects **only** when sourced from platform inventory labels already used elsewhere (e.g., "verified public listings").
- **Forbidden:** Percentages implying returns, rental yields, appreciation rates, or market growth unless citing an approved external source with visible citation — **none approved in Phase 1 inventory**.

## 4. Wording constraints

- Use "educational", "informational", "discovery", "screening", "due diligence".
- Avoid "recommend", "best investment", "high return", "guaranteed", "forecast", "projected yield".
